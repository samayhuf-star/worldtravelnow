Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WorldTravel Now ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website worldtravelnow.online or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our services, including:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number when you contact us or make a booking
  • Passport and travel document details required for flight bookings
  • Payment information (processed securely through encrypted payment processors)
  • Travel preferences and special assistance requests
  • Device information, IP address, browser type, and pages visited

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage flight bookings and travel packages
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send booking confirmations and travel-related notifications
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send promotional offers (only with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Affiliate Disclosure

WorldTravel Now participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click on certain links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us maintain and improve our services.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with airlines, hotels, and tour operators to fulfill your booking, payment processors, legal authorities when required by law, and service providers under strict confidentiality.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

7. Data Security

We implement 256-bit SSL encryption to protect your personal data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@worldtravelnow.online or call (866) 812-0123.
