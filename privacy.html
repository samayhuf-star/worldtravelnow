Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WorldTravel Now ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit worldtravelnow.online or use our services. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our services, including:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number when you contact us or make a booking
  • Passport and travel document details required for flight bookings
  • Payment information (processed securely through encrypted payment processors)
  • Travel preferences and special assistance requests
  • Device information, IP address, browser type, and pages visited (automatically collected)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and manage flight bookings and travel packages
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send booking confirmations and travel-related notifications
  • Improve our website performance and user experience
  • Send promotional offers (only with your consent)
  • Serve relevant advertisements through third-party ad networks
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (session management, security).
  • Analytics Cookies: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This helps us improve our content and user experience. Data collected is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Advertising Cookies: We use Google Ads (formerly Google AdWords) to display relevant advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.
  • Affiliate Tracking Cookies: We use Travelpayouts affiliate cookies to track flight search referrals. These cookies do not store personally identifiable information.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website. You can also opt out of interest-based advertising at aboutads.info or networkadvertising.org.

5. Google Advertising Disclosure

We use Google Ads to promote our services. As part of this:

  • Google may use cookies to serve ads to you based on your past visits to worldtravelnow.online or other websites on the internet.
  • We use Google Remarketing features — this means users who have visited our site may see our ads on other Google partner websites.
  • We comply with Google's advertising policies, including the EU User Consent Policy where applicable.
  • You can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

6. Affiliate Disclosure

WorldTravel Now participates in the Travelpayouts affiliate program. When you click on flight search links and complete a booking, we may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend services we believe provide genuine value to our users. Our affiliate relationships do not influence our content or the prices shown in flight search results.

7. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Airlines, hotels, and tour operators to fulfill your booking
  • Payment processors under strict security and confidentiality agreements
  • Google (for analytics and advertising purposes — see Section 5)
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable law
  • Service providers who assist our operations under strict confidentiality agreements

8. Data Security

We implement 256-bit SSL encryption and industry-standard security protocols to protect your personal data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Right to opt out of marketing communications
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • Right to data portability (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@worldtravelnow.online or call (866) 660-0539.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
